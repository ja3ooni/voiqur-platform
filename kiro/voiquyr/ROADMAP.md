# EUVoice AI Platform Roadmap

This document outlines the planned features and improvements for the EUVoice AI Platform.

## Vision

To become the leading open-source voice AI platform for European and multilingual applications, with best-in-class support for low-resource languages and complete EU compliance.

## Release Schedule

- **v1.1** - Q2 2024 (Performance & Optimization)
- **v1.2** - Q3 2024 (Enhanced Features)
- **v2.0** - Q4 2024 (Major Update)
- **v2.1** - Q1 2025 (Enterprise Features)
